About Us

ICEP is a community engagement programme established by the informatics department of TUT, with the goal to assist students who have completed all of their theoretical modules. The purpose of ICEP is to provide real-world experience that enables students to put everything they have learned into action. ICEP is solely made for TUT students who specialize in Business Applications, Software Development and Multimedia.
